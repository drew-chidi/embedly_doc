---
title: "Customer KYC Upgrade - Address Verification"
api: "POST /customers/kyc/address-verification"
description: "This endpoint enables you to complete address verification for customer in order to upgrade the customer's KYC to Tier 3"
---

<ParamField body="customerId" type="string" required>
  The Embedly unique identifier of the customer whose KYC tier is being
  upgraded. **Example:** `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="meterNumber" type="string" required>
  The utility document of the customer's address.
</ParamField>

<ParamField body="houseAddress" type="string" required>
  The address of the customer whose kyc is to be upgraded.
</ParamField>
