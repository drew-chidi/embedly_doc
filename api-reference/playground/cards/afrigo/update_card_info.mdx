---

<ParamField body='customerId' type='string' required>
  the embedly customerId of the respective customer. **Example:**
  `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='walletId' type='string' required>
  The embedly walletId mapped to the customer's wallet. **Example:**
  `3fa85f64-5717-4562-b3fc-2c963f66afa6`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='cardNumber' type='string' required>
  The unique number mapped to the card on the customer's wallet **Example:**
  `1234`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='nameOnCard' type='string' required>
  The name of the customer. **Example:** `1234`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='oldMobile' type='string' required>
  The customer's old phone number. **Example:** `1234`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='newMobile' type='string' required>
  The customer's new phone number. **Example:** `1234`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='email' type='string' required>
  The customer's email address. **Example:** `john@doe.com`
</ParamField>

<ParamField body='address' type='string' required>
  The customer's residential address. **Example:** `20 Marina, Lagos State`
</ParamField>
